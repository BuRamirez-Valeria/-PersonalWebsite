🐢 TURTLE 🐢

Turtles are one of the world's oldest reptile species. They are one-of-a-kind pets due to their rigid shell and slow-moving behaviors. They're tough little critters who can be a lot of fun to look after. Although turtles appear to be low-maintenance pets, most species can live for decades, making them a lifelong commitment.
